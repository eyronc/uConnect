# uConnect

**uConnect** is an all-in-one academic portal designed to simplify the student experience by combining **enrollment, payments, learning resources, and interactive study tools** into a single platform.

The system helps students manage their academic journey while also making learning more engaging through **course-specific mini-games and AI assistance**.

---

## Features

### 🎓 Enrollment & Student Portal

* Online subject **enrollment system**
* **Payment management** and tracking
* Access to **course materials and learning resources**
* Centralized **student dashboard**

### 🎮 Course-Based Mini Games

uConnect includes interactive learning tools designed for different programs:

* **IT / Computer Science** – *1v1 Coding Battles* where students can compete by solving programming challenges.
* **Nursing** – *Flashcard Review System* for memorizing medical terms, procedures, and concepts.
* **Accounting** – *Financial Scenario Quizzes* that help students practice bookkeeping and financial decision-making.
* **Tourism** – *Destination & Travel Simulation Activities* to learn about tourism management and planning.
* **Engineering** – *Problem-Solving Challenges* focused on logic, calculations, and engineering concepts.

### 🤖 AI MCP Chatbot

An **AI-powered MCP chatbot** assists students by:

* Answering academic and portal-related questions
* Helping students navigate the system
* Providing study support and explanations
